Welcome to the House of Parliament's Webcast reporting page

This reporting page can provide you with all the information necessary to understand the effectiveness
and reach of your On-Demand Webcasts

The Webcast reporting dashboard is divided into the following sections:

  • Site Data
  • Events Specific
  • Support Specific
  • Rating Specific

The Site data section is referring to the total amount of users that have visited the site within a specified time interval, using the information contained in this section you will be able to identify intervals of time where users are more likely to use your site and what type of devices are using to access your webcasts, this will help you with creating content targeted for specific type of audiences

The Events data section is referring to the total amount of users that have visited Webcasts within a specified time interval, differently from the "Site Data" section, this report shows you the number of visitors to the Webcasts and gives you the opportunity to drill down into a specific single Webcast to observe how participants have connected to it and the browser utilized, this will help you with creating content targeted for specific type of audiences
