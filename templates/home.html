Welcome to the House of Parliament's Webcast reporting page

This reporting page can provide you with all the information necessary to understand the effectiveness
and reach of your On-Demand Webcasts

The Webcast reporting dashboard is divided into the following sections:

  • Site Data
  • Events Specific
  • Support Specific
  • Rating Specific

The Site data section is referring to the total amount of users that have visited the site within a specified time interval, using the information contained in this section you will be able to identify intervals of time where users are more likely to use your site and what type of devices are using to access your webcasts, this will help you with creating content targeted for specific type of audiences.

The Events specific section is referring to the total amount of users that have visited Webcasts within a specified time interval, differently from the "Site Data" section, this report shows you the number of visitors to the Webcasts and gives you the opportunity to drill down into a specific single Webcast to observe how participants have connected to it and the browser utilized, this will help you with creating content targeted for specific type of audiences.

The Support specific section will give you the opportunity to see how your webcasts are performing in regards of "technical issues", every ticket submitted is being recorded and shown as an accumulation of requests in this section, you can see the total number of requests, or select a single webcast, this will enable you to understand eventual issues with client configuration or security settings.

The Rating specific section will provide you with information on how each of your webcast is performing with the audience and what is the user's feedback on that specific webcast, this will help you understand what content is most liked and identify your audience's preferences.
